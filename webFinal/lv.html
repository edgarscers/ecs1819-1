Latvian

Latvia is a country in North-Eastern Europe, sharing borders with Estonia, Lithuania, Belarus, and Russia. This year, the country celebrated its 100th year of independence. The size of the country is 24,938 sq miles and the population is under 2 milion.

Culture

Food

There are many aspects to the Latvian culture. Latvians love to sing and dance. Many participate in choirs or folk dancing groups. Every two years, Latvia hosts the Song and Dance Festival. This past summer, Latvia celebrated its 100th year of independence as well as the Song and Dance Fesitval.

Food in Latvia's resturants mainly is served in buffets style. Potatoes and bread are two very popular food items in Latvia. Soups are also very popular, including Beetroot soup. The food in Latvia is very delicious.
